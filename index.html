why use expenses?

expenses is a calculator designed to help you spend and stack your money responsibly. expenses makes recommendations based on your membership plan to guide you through purchases big or small.

who is expenses for?

expenses comes with two plans built with anyone in mind. Our Super Stackers plan for members with a smaller spending budgets -- and our Ultra Stacker plan, to help balance and manage bigger budgets. No matter what we all have expenses.
